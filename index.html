About Formula One
Formula One (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing, governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It features the fastest and most technologically advanced cars in the world, competing in a series of races called Grands Prix, held on circuits and city streets across the globe. F1 combines elite driving skill, cutting-edge engineering, and strategic team management, making it one of the most prestigious and watched motorsports worldwide.
